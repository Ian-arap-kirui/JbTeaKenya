Join the Future of Food at Gulfood 2025!

  • By admin
  • Events
  • January 29th

JBTea Kenya Ltd is proud to participate in Gulfood 2025, the world’s largest annual food and beverage trade exhibition, taking place from February 17–21, 2025, at the Dubai World Trade Centre. This landmark 30th edition will bring together global industry leaders, buyers, investors, and food innovators, shaping the future of food trade and business.

At Gulfood 2025, over 5,500 exhibitors from 129+ countries will showcase cutting-edge products, emerging food trends, and groundbreaking solutions revolutionizing the global food supply chain. This year introduces new participants from Kosovo, Madagascar, Mauritius, and Zambia, adding to the dynamic mix of international flavors and expertise.

As a premier Kenyan tea producer, JBTea Kenya Ltd is excited to present our finest selection of premium teas, highlighting Kenya’s reputation as a leading global tea exporter. Visitors will get the opportunity to explore our rich flavors, sustainable sourcing methods, and innovative tea blends tailored for today’s discerning consumers.

Gulfood 2025 is more than just an exhibition—it’s a platform for networking, learning, and driving business growth. Industry experts will engage in high-level discussions, product launches, and investment opportunities, offering unparalleled insights into the next frontier of food and beverage trade.

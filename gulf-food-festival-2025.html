Join the Future of Food at Gulfood 2025!

  • By admin
  • Events
  • January 29th

JBTea Kenya Ltd is thrilled to participate in Gulfood 2025, the world’s largest annual food and beverage trade exhibition, taking place from February 17–21, 2025, at the Dubai World Trade Centre. This milestone 30th edition will unite industry leaders, buyers, investors, and innovators to shape the future of food trade.

Featuring 5,500+ exhibitors from 129+ countries, Gulfood 2025 is the ultimate platform to explore cutting-edge products, emerging trends, and sustainable solutions. First-time participants include Kosovo, Madagascar, Mauritius, and Zambia, enriching the event’s diverse global presence.

As a Kenyan tea producer, JBTea Kenya Ltd will showcase its finest premium teas, emphasizing authentic flavors, sustainable farming, and innovative tea blends tailored for global consumers. Experience the excellence of Kenyan tea and discover how we are redefining quality in the industry.

Why Attend Gulfood 2025?

  • ✅ Discover Global Food Trends – Explore innovations driving the next decade of the industry.
  • ✅ Network with Industry Leaders – Connect with top buyers, suppliers, and investors.
  • ✅ Experience Kenyan Tea Excellence – Taste premium teas crafted for international markets.
  • ✅ Unlock Business Opportunities – Expand your reach and establish valuable partnerships.

Join us at Gulfood 2025 and be part of the future of food and beverage trade!

"Gulfood 2025 is a transformative event that sets the pace for food innovation, trade, and sustainability. We are honored to represent Kenya’s tea industry on this global stage."

Dr. Jaswinder Benawra (OGW), Director

Meet Us at Gulfood 2025!

Visit our booth at the Dubai World Trade Centre from February 17–21, 2025, and explore our premium tea collection. Let’s shape the future of food together!

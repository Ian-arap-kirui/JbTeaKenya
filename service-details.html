Sourcing and Sustainability

Tea plantations

Our tea is sourced from the fertile highlands of Kenya, where the climate and soil conditions are ideal for growing the finest tea. We work closely with local farmers to ensure sustainable and ethical farming practices.


Sustainability practices

We are dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint. Our initiatives include organic farming, eco-friendly packaging, and a commitment to reducing carbon emissions throughout our supply chain.


Transparency

We believe in transparency at every step of the journey. From the moment the tea leaves are plucked to the time they are packaged, we ensure full traceability and accountability.



Export & Distribution

Global reach

We export to over 30 countries worldwide, including the United States, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. Our global network ensures timely and reliable delivery to your doorstep.


Efficient logistics

Partnering with leading logistics companies, we guarantee fast and secure shipping. Whether you need a small shipment or a full container, we have the capacity and expertise to meet your requirements.



Custom packaging solutions

Choose from a variety of packaging options tailored to your specifications, whether you require bulk packaging, retail-ready packs, or custom-branded solutions. We ensure your tea reaches you in pristine condition.
